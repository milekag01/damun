DAMUNC DAIICT, Gandhinagar

Letter from SEC-GEN

Dear Delegates,

I am absolutely honoured to invite you all to the first ever edition of the Dhirubhai Ambani Model United Nations Conference!

DAMUNC shall uphold its high standard of invigorating debate and enriching cultural exchange experiences. Above all, however, DAMUNC will try its best to be a launchpad for the future thinkers, leaders and policy makers, thereby preparing them to resolve the most exigent global issues. Amidst teaching the intricacies of economic sanctions and global political history, we will be demonstrating a lifetime skill that transcends the silos of any classroom: diplomacy. Eloquently quoted by America’s 44th President, Barack Obama, “In a world of complex threats, our security and leadership depends on all elements of our power - including strong and principled diplomacy.”

This conference will take place from 5 th October 2019 to 6 th October 2019 at DA-IICT, Gandhinagar. The college stands up as the top technical institute in Gujarat and has ties with several government organizations.

The members of the DAMUNC OC and Secretariat are working diligently to bring life to riveting committee, plan dynamic delegate events and improve the conference to be both competitive and educational. We are excited to expand conference initiatives to offer an enhanced experience to the delegates through increased staffer participation and greater technological involvements.

I encourage all interested delegates to peruse through our website for more information. The entire DAMUNC OC, Secretariat and I could not be more excited to host you in DA-IICT and welcome you the starting of a new tradition and culture.

Best Wishes,
Vyom Saraf
Secretary-General, DAMUNC 2019
